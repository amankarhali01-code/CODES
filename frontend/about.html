About Me

I am Amandeep Singh from Patiala, Punjab, India. I completed my Senior Secondary education in 2023 with Arts stream, and now I am pursuing Bachelor of Computer Applications (2024-2027).

My journey into technology started from a turning point. After facing rejection from Bachelor in Psychology, I decided not to stop and instead chose BCA. That decision changed my direction and mindset.

Transitioning from Arts to Technology was challenging in the beginning, but it taught me adaptability, discipline, and consistent self-learning. I worked to build confidence in programming concepts, frontend development, and real-world problem solving.

Today, my goal is clear: become a professional web developer and eventually build a tech startup. I believe in long-term growth, practical learning, and creating products that can make a strong impact.
